Why use Heal

  • When you're sick
  • When you're well

Cold and cough

Cold and cough

When you’re sick with a cold or a nasty cough the last thing you want to do is get out of bed or leave the house. Heal will connect you with a great doctor right away.

Fever, flu or headache

Fever, flu or headache

Whether it’s the flu, a fever or just a bad headache, Heal can connect you with a doctor to save the day.

Rash

Rash

Don’t wait to get that rash looked at. Request a caring, licensed physician using Heal to give you peace of mind.

Ear infection

Ear infection

Earaches can really hurt. Trust us, we hear you! That’s why we’ve made sure the doctors on Heal are equipped with all of the tools they need to diagnose and prescribe treatment for earaches or infections.

Sore throat or strep

Sore throat or strep

Having a sore throat (or maybe even strep throat) is the worst. It’s hard to get anything done when just trying to swallow hurts. Let the doctors on Heal help you get some relief.

Sports injuries

Sports injuries

Whether you’re a weekend warrior or elite athlete, we all get sore or injured. The doctors on Heal can assess your sports injury and help you through it so you can get back out there as soon as possible.

Stomach aches

Stomach aches

No one likes tummy trouble. If you’re experiencing stomach, digestive or other lower abdominal pain, use Heal to request a qualified doctor to come examine you.

Well-baby exam

Well-baby exam

There’s nothing better than having a beautiful, bouncing baby. Use Heal to request a caring, fully-vetted pediatrician to give your baby a check-up and make sure they’re progressing just as they should be.

Annual physical

Annual physical

Has it been more than a year since you had your annual physical exam? Don’t wait to get a check-up. Use Heal to request a doctor to come to you and perform a full physical.

Sports physical

Sports physical

Need to get ready for the season? Use Heal to get a great doctor to come and make sure you’re ready to perform at the highest level. Heal is for athletes too.

Camp physical

Camp physical

Time to send the little one off to camp? Get them checked out by a great pediatrician so you know they’ll be happy and healthy while they’re off roasting marshmallows and making memories.

School physical

School physical

Don’t let your child’s school physical paperwork become your overdue homework. Request a high-quality doctor to come to your home and examine your child when it’s convenient for you.

Flu shots

Flu shots

Don’t let the flu get you. Use Heal to get a flu shot for you or your child to stay protected.

Family health assessment

Family health assessment

Take care of the ones who matter most with a Heal Family Health Assessment. A family practice doctor will come to your home and make sure your family is eating right, getting enough exercise and look out for anything to keep an eye on.

Primary care physician

Primary care physician

Haven’t had a doctor for a while or just moved and haven’t had a chance to find one? That’s okay. Let Heal help find you a great doctor to see you when you’re sick and when you’re well.
